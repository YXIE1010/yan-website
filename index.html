About me

Hello, welcome to my personal website. I am a PhD Candidate at University of Michigan with my research focused on hydrometeorology in the Arctic. Our recent research, advised by Prof. Claire Pettersen and Prof. Mark Flanner, investigates rainfall events and their impacts on accelerating surface snow melt at high latitudes. Meanwhile, I am developing a machine-learning based approach to further examine rainfall variations.
